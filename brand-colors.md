# Brand Color Palette Analysis

Based on the Physio At Your Doorstep logo:

## Primary Colors

1. **Navy Blue** (House/Border)
   - Hex: #3B3B6D (approximate)
   - RGB: rgb(59, 59, 109)
   - HSL: hsl(240, 30%, 33%)
   - Usage: Primary brand color, headers, main elements

2. **Red** (Letter P)
   - Hex: #E31E24 (approximate)
   - RGB: rgb(227, 30, 36)
   - HSL: hsl(358, 77%, 50%)
   - Usage: Accent color, CTAs, highlights

3. **Black** (Stethoscope/Text)
   - Hex: #1A1A1A
   - RGB: rgb(26, 26, 26)
   - Usage: Text, icons, details

4. **White** (Background)
   - Hex: #FFFFFF
   - Usage: Backgrounds, contrast

## Color Strategy

- **Primary**: Navy Blue (#3B3B6D) - Professional, trustworthy, medical
- **Accent**: Red (#E31E24) - Energy, urgency, call-to-action
- **Text**: Black/Dark Gray - Readability
- **Background**: White/Light Gray - Clean, medical feel

## Implementation Plan

1. Replace teal/green with navy blue as primary
2. Use red for CTAs and important highlights
3. Maintain high contrast for accessibility
4. Keep white backgrounds for clean medical aesthetic
