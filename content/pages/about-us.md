## Meet Our Founder

### Dr. Atharva Mishra — Chief Physiotherapist

Dr. Atharva Mishra is the Founder and Chief Physiotherapist at "**Physio At Your Doorstep**", which is a platform that provides physiotherapy care to people at the comfort of their home throughout Bangalore.

He specializes in ACL Rehabilitation, Post-Surgical Physiotherapy, Post-Operative stiffness, Sports Injuries, Shoulder Rehabilitation, Manual Therapy and mobilization.

He is familiar with a wide range of techniques and approaches which provides in-depth opportunities for him to treat a variety of diseases by means of an integrated and holistic approach.

Dr. Atharva is very passionate to help people come out of their long-standing pain. He feels that constant nagging pain not only causes physical discomfort but it also results in a lot of irritation and low energy. His philosophy is that people are more fulfilled and happy in both professional and personal life if they are free of pain.

He founded "Physio At Your Doorstep" so that he can provide the best care to each and every individual at the comfort of home.

#### Credentials and Qualifications

He has completed his Bachelors of Physiotherapy (BPT) from Govt. Physiotherapy College, Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India, affiliated to the Rajasthan University of Health Sciences (RUHS) and Masters in Sports Physiotherapy from Jaipur Physiotherapy College which is affiliated to Maharaj Vinayak Global University (MVGU).

Dr. Atharva Mishra has done numerous certified training courses, such as Dry Needling (DNT), Kinesiotaping (KTP), Kinetic Chain Activation Technique (KCAT) and BFRT.

#### Other Expertise

- Stroke Rehabilitation
- Manual Therapy and Mobilisation
- Vestibular Rehabilitation
- Spinal Manipulation
