A typical home physiotherapy session usually lasts about **45 minutes to 1 hour**. Subsequent sessions are often 45 minutes each, focusing on exercises, stretches, and therapy techniques tailored to you. The **number of sessions** you'll need depends on your specific condition and goals. Some patients achieve their goals in those sessions, while others might continue with additional sessions or a maintenance program for long-term conditions. Frequency of sessions per week can also vary, often it's about 2-3 sessions per week to start (allowing rest days for recovery), but in some cases daily sessions are recommended (e.g., immediately post-surgery or in intensive rehab situations).
