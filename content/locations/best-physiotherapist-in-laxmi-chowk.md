### How should I prepare for an at-home physiotherapy visit?

Preparing for a home physio session is easy and doesn't require any special equipment in most cases. Here are a few tips to get ready:

- **Choose a comfortable space:** Clear a small area in your home for the session – typically a living room or bedroom with enough floor space. Move aside any fragile or obstructive items. If you have an exercise mat or a firm bed, that can be useful for exercises done lying down.
- **Wear comfortable clothing:** Put on loose-fitting, stretchable clothes that allow free movement (e.g. a t-shirt and shorts or track pants). This lets you exercise without restriction and allows the therapist to evaluate your movement properly.
- **Keep basic items handy:** Have a bottle of water and a towel nearby. If you have any prior medical reports or a doctor's referral related to your condition, keep those ready to show the physiotherapist. Also, if you use any assistive devices (like a cane, walker, or knee brace), mention it to the therapist so they can incorporate it into your session if needed.
